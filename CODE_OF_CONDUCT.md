# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in AgentEase a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Respectful communication
- Constructive feedback
- Inclusive language

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting comments
- Public or private harassment

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

## Reporting

Report issues to the repository maintainers.

---

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant.
